The **eXtreme Concentration AI Observatory (AIonXC)** is a civic technology platform designed to empower global citizens to actively participate in the governance of artificial intelligence. Our mission is to provide transparency into the concentration of power within the AI industry and to equip individuals with the tools to influence corporate behavior through coordinated capital allocation.

We believe that the future of AI should not be decided by a handful of powerful entities. By democratizing market influence, we can collectively steer the development of AI towards a more equitable, ethical, and beneficial future for all of humanity.

## üõ°Ô∏è Security & Trust: Your Transactions are Secure

We understand that trust is paramount, especially when financial transactions are involved. The AI Observatory is built with security as a top priority, ensuring that your participation is safe and your data is protected.

### Transaction Security
- **Broker Integration:** We do not handle your money directly. All transactions are securely processed through established, regulated, and reputable brokerage firms. We simply provide an interface to initiate actions through your own broker account.
- **Secure Connections:** All connections to brokerage APIs are encrypted and follow industry-standard security protocols (OAuth 2.0, API keys).
- **No Custody of Assets:** At no point does AIonXC take custody of your funds or securities. You retain full control over your brokerage accounts.

### Data Privacy
- **Minimal Data Collection:** We collect only the minimum data necessary for the platform to function. We do not track personal investment portfolios or sensitive financial information.
- **Anonymized Analytics:** All platform-wide data, such as the "Approve vs. Disapprove Race Tracker," is aggregated and anonymized to protect individual user privacy.
- **Compliance:** We are committed to complying with global data protection regulations, including GDPR and CCPA. Our detailed Privacy Policy is always accessible.

### Permissions & Compliance
- **Regulated Partners:** We partner exclusively with licensed and regulated financial institutions to ensure all transactions comply with financial laws and regulations.
- **Adherence to Standards:** Our platform adheres to the highest standards of security and data protection to qualify for monetization programs like Google AdSense, ensuring a sustainable model for our civic mission.
